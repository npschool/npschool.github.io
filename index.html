About Us

A School should be a place of Light, of Liberty and of Learning

Nagarjuna Public School is the oldest institution since 1983, located beside NH 216, Ramanagaram. It provides education to all the students namely, Kinder garten to SSC. The medium of instruction is given in English (EM) and Telugu (TM).

We aims at the all round development of the child keeping in mind the age wise requirement or the need. It’s an institution were character is built, a fountain head where children can constantly seek creative expressions for their endeavors. The school strongly emphasizes in the development of child through professional approach, management principles and modern technology.
